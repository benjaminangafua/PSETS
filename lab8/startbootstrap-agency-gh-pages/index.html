About

We are young Liberians providing job for our friends who are skilled but finding it diffiulty to get jobs or contracts.

  • 2019-2020

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Providing quality education in rural areas in Liberia

  • March 2019

    Lunch the Idea of Skilled Outreach

    We started with educating K-12 students to understand human needs in all fields. This got us ready to established this initiative.

  • December 2019

    Transition to Full Service

    We carried out series of surveys to see the number of unemployed youth in the county (Lofa, Liberia).

  • July 2020

    Phase Two Expansion

    At this time, we got fully settle to start our planning for posting on social medias to advertise our goals(Getting the youth to earn their living).
