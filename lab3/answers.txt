sort1 uses:  Bubble sort.
How do you know?
Using the time function, bubble sort perform faster when number are already sorted.
Because it compares two arrays at a time. And swipe them in their order if necessary.

sort2 uses: Merge sort
How do you know?
This takes arrays and divide into left and right halves.
After that, it sort each half recursively and afterward, we merge them.

sort3 uses: Selection sort.
How do you know?
Whether sorted or not, selection sort go through the entire  array of numbers one by one.
